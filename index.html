About Heroz Driving School

Founded in 2010, Heroz Driving School has been helping Edmonton residents become safe and confident drivers for over a decade. Our mission is to provide high-quality driver education that emphasizes safety, responsibility, and skill.

All our instructors are certified by Alberta Transportation and have undergone rigorous training to ensure they can provide the best possible instruction to our students.

We pride ourselves on our patient, professional approach and our commitment to helping every student reach their full potential as a driver.
